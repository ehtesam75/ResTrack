Points System

Lifetime points are calculated based on exam performance and achievement bonuses. Points have real monetary value and can be withdrawn as rewards!

üí∞ Points = Real Money!

1 Point = 1 BDT (Bangladeshi Taka)

Every point you earn in ResTrack has real monetary value. Your academic achievements directly translate to financial rewards!

üíµ How Withdrawals Work:

  • If you have more than 0 points remaining, you may request a withdrawal
  • The amount (in BDT) will be provided by your teacher/admin
  • Spent Points: Points you've already withdrawn or used
  • Remaining Points: Points available for withdrawal (your current balance)

Example: If you have 100 lifetime points and spent 30, you have 70 points (70 BDT) available to withdraw!

Exam Points

Each exam contributes points based on the achieved grade. The cumulative sum of all exam points forms the base of your lifetime total.

Example: Scoring 88% on a CQ exam (Superb grade) awards +20 points (20 BDT), while 45% on an MCQ exam (Poor grade) results in -10 points (-10 BDT).

Bonus Points

You can earn additional bonus points (extra BDT!) through special achievements like monthly wins.

Total Lifetime Points

Formula: Lifetime Points = Exam Points + Bonus Points

Lifetime points determine leaderboard position and reflect overall academic performance throughout the tracking period.

Monthly Wins Bonus

Students who achieve first rank in any completed month receive a performance bonus.

40 Points per Monthly Win

Each month where a student achieves first rank (highest average score) earns a 40-point bonus added to their lifetime total.

Monthly Ranking Methodology

  • Only completed months are evaluated (current month excluded)
  • Average score is calculated for all exams within the month
  • The student with the highest monthly average is declared the winner
  • Each monthly win awards +40 bonus points

Example: Achieving first rank in January, February, and April results in 3 √ó 40 = 120 bonus points added to the lifetime total.

Automatic Calculation

Monthly wins bonus is calculated automatically whenever:

  • A new exam is added or edited
  • An exam is deleted
  • An admin recalculates points

Key Terminology

Average Score

Your weighted average across all exams. Calculated as: (Total Marks Obtained √∑ Total Possible Marks) √ó 100%

Exam Points

Points earned (or lost) based on your grade in each individual exam. Ranges from -20 to +20 per exam.

Lifetime Points

Total accumulated points from all exams plus any bonus points.

Bonus Points

Additional points earned through achievements like monthly wins. Currently: 40 points per monthly #1 ranking.

Best Month

The month where you achieved your highest average score. Displayed in your student profile to highlight peak performance.

Bulk Entry

Feature that allows adding multiple exam records at once for all students in a single operation, saving time when entering exam results.

Monthly Wins

The number of completed months where you achieved the #1 rank (highest average score). Each win = 40 bonus points.

Subject Tops

The number of subjects where you hold rank #1. Displayed in your student profile to show how many subjects you are topping.
